31 Gram Gold Vein in Quartz Specimen - Western Australia

 Sold out 
This gorgeous Australian gold in quartz specimen from Western Australia has distinct veins of crystalline gold running through it. An ohmmeter shows that the veins of gold are running all the way through the specimen so there is a lot of gold inside this beauty. I don't think you'd want to crush it though because it is really amazing to look at and would look great in your gold specimen collection. The weight is 31.1 Grams, exactly one troy ounce. The quartz is multi-colored, with white, tan, medium to dark brown, and a light rose tint with layers of black graphitic slate type material. It is very rare and unique. It was found during a prospecting expedition in the mid 1990's. The size is 1.5" x 1.25" x 3/4". This is a very beautiful and interesting gold in quartz specimen
